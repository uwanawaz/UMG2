About Us

Unique Maritime (Group of Companies), was established in the year 1990 with the desire to handle all shipping activities including Liner and Tramp vessels. It is now one of the stentorian companies of The Ocean Group. Unique Maritime Agencies, is involved in commercial shipping since 1st September 2000. UNIQUE GROUP is a leading provider of end-to-end supply chain solutions To/From Pakistan demonstrating core competencies in domestic and international freight transportation, integrated logistics management and information technology. Our strong financial position, global network, partnerships and solid operational infrastructure have allowed us to rise to the top of the list of Logistic providers in Pakistan.

Our Affiliations

Proudly affiliated with the top organizations worldwide!

The International Federation of Freight Forwarders Association - FIATA

Our affiliation with The International Federation of Freight Forwarders Associations (FIATA) is a testament to our commitment to global excellence in logistics. It provides us with a vast global network, access to industry resources, and ensures we adhere to international standards. This affiliation boosts customer confidence, enhances our team's skills, and positions us as leaders in the industry, dedicated to innovation and best practices.

Pakistan International Freight Forwarders Association - PIFFA

Our affiliation with the Pakistan International Freight Forwarders Association (PIFFA) underscores our dedication to the logistics industry in Pakistan. It grants us access to a valuable local network, industry expertise, and resources. PIFFA membership enhances our capabilities, ensuring we deliver top-notch services and adhere to national standards. This affiliation reflects our commitment to serving the Pakistani logistics sector and continuously improving our offerings.

Karachi Chamber of Commerce and Industry - KCCI

Our affiliation with the Karachi Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI) highlights our strong connection to the business community in Karachi, Pakistan. It provides us with access to valuable local resources, business insights, and networking opportunities. KCCI membership enhances our ability to contribute to the region's economic growth and engage in meaningful collaborations. This affiliation demonstrates our commitment to the business landscape in Karachi and our dedication to fostering economic development in the area.

Federal Maritime Commission Competition and Integrity for America's Supply Chain

Our affiliation with the Federal Maritime Commission (FMC) is a testament to our commitment to upholding the highest standards in the maritime industry. The FMC is the regulatory body overseeing ocean transportation and trade practices in the United States, and our partnership with them underscores our dedication to transparency, compliance, and fair business practices. Being affiliated with the FMC means that we adhere to their regulations and guidelines rigorously. This not only ensures that we meet all legal requirements but also demonstrates our willingness to go above and beyond to provide our customers with the utmost confidence in our services.
